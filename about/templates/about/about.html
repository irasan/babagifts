About Us

BabaGifts is a family run business based in Dublin, Ireland. It was founded back in 2014 when our beautiful daughter Leila was born. We wanted to dress her in pretty clothes and matching accessories to show her off to the world. We also cared about the quality of the things we put on her and preferred less items that would last for longer instead of mass shopping of poor quality items.
The shoes and hairbands that were available for babies fell short of both fabric quality and design, and we were left searching the internet.

Herein started the journey of BabaGifts..
We handcraft shoes, hair bows, crowns and more from the softest leather. All our items are made to order to best match our clients' needs. Please feel free to send us an email with any special requirements or questions you may have.
