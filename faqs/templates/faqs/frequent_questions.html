Frequently Asked Questions

What is BabaGifts?

We started BabaGifts to create a fun, lasting experience for mamas and their little ones everywhere. We love bows and every other cute thing we can get our hands on! Currently we offer products ranging from bows to shoes and other cute accessories. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we do!

What are the perks of beeing a monthly subscriber?

Monthly subscribers not only have first access to our newest and exclusive products, but they also get them at a much cheaper price plus free shipping!

How much is shipping fee?

Bows, pacifier clips and crowns are shipped for €3 while shoes will be shipped for €5.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards.

Do you accept returns

All our products are made to order and we can guarantee their quality. This is the reason why we don't accept returns

Where are your products made?

We handcraft our products is a studio in Dublin, Ireland.
